## Best Practices

1. **Personalize first line** — reference their portfolio or tweets
2. **Keep under 150 words** — VCs skim
3. **Include one clear CTA** — "20 min call this week?"
4. **Attach nothing** — link to deck instead
5. **Follow up once** after 5-7 days, then move on
6. **Track everything** in spreadsheet
7. **Email Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am UK time**
