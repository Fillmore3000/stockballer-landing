# StockBaller — Investor FAQ

## Business Model

### Q: How do you make money?
**A:** Three revenue streams:
1. **Trading fees (1%)** — on every buy/sell transaction
2. **IPO spreads (5%)** — when we launch new athlete tokens
3. **Premium subscriptions** — advanced analytics, alerts (future)
4. **B2B white-label licensing** — platform + oracle licensing (starts Q1 2027)

At £1M monthly volume, that's ~£10K monthly revenue from fees alone.

---

### Q: What's the unit economics?
**A:** Projected:
- Avg trades per user/month: 10.6
- Avg trade value: £145
- Monthly volume per user: £1,537
- Fee revenue per user/month (1%): ~£15.37
- Baseline OpEx model: £510/month (<1,000 users)
- Break-even: ~34 active users

---

### Q: Why would users keep trading?
**A:** Three engagement loops:
1. **Match days** — prices move, creates urgency
2. **Portfolio building** — diversification motivation
3. **Yield mechanics** — future staking/dividends

Unlike betting (lose = churn), our users build positions over time.

## Regulatory & Legal

### Q: Is this gambling?
**A:** No. Key differences:
| Gambling | StockBaller |
|----------|-------------|
| Bet on outcomes (win/lose) | Own asset with gradual price changes |
| Time-limited (90 mins) | Hold indefinitely |
| House edge | Peer-to-peer market |
| Binary result | Continuous valuation |

We're closer to "trading" than "betting."

---

### Q: Do you need an FCA license?
**A:** TBD based on legal classification:
1. **Crypto asset** — falls under incoming UK crypto regulations
2. **Financial instrument** — would need MiFID authorization
3. **Gaming/skill** — lighter touch regulation

We're budgeting £40K for legal opinion and potential FCA sandbox application.

---

### Q: What about athlete likeness rights?
**A:** Three approaches:
1. **Stats-based only** — prices derived from public match data (defensible)
2. **League partnerships** — future B2B deals for official licensing
3. **DAO governance** — community decides which athletes to list

Initial launch uses publicly available performance data only.

## Technical

### Q: Why Base (Coinbase L2)?
**A:** 
1. **Low fees** — <£0.01 per transaction vs £5+ on Ethereum
2. **Coinbase trust** — mainstream users already have Coinbase
3. **USDC native** — seamless stablecoin integration
4. **Ecosystem support** — grants, partnerships, BD help

---

### Q: How do prices update?
**A:** Oracle-based pricing:
1. API-Football provides real-time match stats
2. Backend + Chainlink CRE workflow calculate new prices using formula
3. Verified updates are written on-chain (Base Sepolia in current deployment)
4. Users see refreshed prices shortly after data updates

Flow is automated, with manual override controls retained for operations safety.

---

### Q: What if someone manipulates prices?
**A:** Mitigations:
1. **Oracle-controlled** — users can't set prices
2. **Fixed supply** — can't infinite mint
3. **Rate limits** — max transactions per second
4. **Trading fees** — wash trading is expensive

Long-term: decentralized oracle (Chainlink integration).

---

### Q: How do you handle liquidity?
**A:** 
1. **Fixed supply (1000 tokens per athlete)** — scarcity
2. **Price discovery** — oracle sets fair price, removes spread risk
3. **Platform as market maker** — we hold inventory initially
4. **No AMM slippage** — fixed price per transaction
