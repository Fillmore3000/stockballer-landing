# StockBaller - Growth Strategy and Projections

## Scope
This document aligns product, go-to-market, operating model, and financial projections from April 2026 through Q4 2029.

## Strategic Objective
Build a frequent, loyal user base and scale from sub-1,000 users to 10,000 users while adding recurring B2B revenue.

## Product Retention Priorities
1. Reduce onboarding friction (fiat-to-USDC flow).
2. Create daily habit loops (watchlists and event alerts).
3. Add social competition (public portfolios and leaderboards).
4. Increase catalog depth (25 -> 100+ players over time).
5. Launch user-vs-AI prediction loop to improve repeat usage.

## Operating Model (Bots + Humans)

### Always-on automated agents
- Market Maker Fleet: liquidity and baseline market activity.
- Price Oracle Agent: API-Football ingest + price updates via oracle flow.
- Prediction Engine Agent: GPT-based pre-match prediction generation.
- Settlement Watcher: completed fixture checks and settlement triggers.
- Alert Dispatcher Agent: user push alerts for events and price moves.
- Roster Sync Agent: ID mapping checks and data quality flags.

### Human core team progression
- 2026: Founder/CEO, CTO, 1 full-stack engineer, part-time growth support.
- 2027: Add Data Quality Lead, Community Manager, fractional Legal/Compliance, Partnerships lead.
- 2028: Add Product Manager and Support lead.
- 2029: Expand engineering and operations for multi-region scale.

## Revenue Model
1. Trading fees: 1.0 percent per buy/sell transaction.
2. Premium subscriptions: analytics, alerts, advanced features.
3. B2B white-label licensing: starts Q1 2027.

## Projection Assumptions
- Monthly volume per active user: £1,537.
- Fee revenue per user per month: about £15.37 at 1.0 percent fee.
- Baseline small-scale OpEx starts near prior model levels and increases with team/infrastructure growth.
- B2B starts in Q1 2027 and ramps with pilot conversions.

## B2B White-Label Plan (Starts Q1 2027)

### Offer structure
1. Setup + deployment package.
2. Monthly platform licensing.
3. Optional revenue-share tier.
4. Optional managed oracle and analytics support.

### Rollout
- Q1 2027: 1-2 pilot design partners.
- Q2-Q4 2027: 3-5 active clients.
- 2028: productized package and vertical expansion.
- 2029: international scaling and larger strategic contracts.

## Key Milestones
- Q2 2026: Mainnet launch and first 500 users.
- Q4 2026: 2,500-user trajectory validated.
- Q1 2027: First B2B pilot live.
- Q4 2028: 5,500 users reached.
- Q4 2029: 10,000 users reached.

## Risks and Mitigations
1. Data quality and latency risk.
Mitigation: supplier SLA, fallback source, audit logs.

2. Regulatory uncertainty risk.
Mitigation: staged legal workstream and compliance budget.

3. User growth variance risk.
Mitigation: social loops, referral mechanics, event-based campaigns.

4. Infrastructure scaling risk.
Mitigation: staged OpEx expansion and reliability SLO tracking.
