## What Are Athlete Tokens?

Athlete tokens are digital assets recorded on the blockchain that represent a stake in a player's performance. Unlike traditional sports betting, these tokens aren't about predicting match outcomes—they're about investing in talent over time.

When a player scores goals, provides assists, keeps clean sheets, or earns player-of-the-match awards, their token value increases. It's like the stock market, but for football.
