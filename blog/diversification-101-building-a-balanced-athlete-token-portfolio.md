## Pillar 1: The Blue-Chip Anchors (The 70%)

Your portfolio needs a foundation. In the Stockballer ecosystem, **Blue-Chip Athletes** are the equivalent of S&P 500 stocks. These are established world-class performers: the Kevin De Bruynes, the Harry Kanes, the Mohamed Salahs.

*   **Risk Profile:** Low.
*   **Volatility:** Sub-**2.5%**.
*   **The Play:** Consistent yield and price stability.

Why hold these? Because when the market gets shaky, these players retain value. Their "floor" is high because their performance is predictable. They are the engines that drive your portfolio's base value. 

🚀 **Action:** Allocate **70%** of your capital here. Build your base. Protect your downside. Give your **Growth** positions room to run.

## Pillar 2: The Growth Assets (The 20%)

This is your upside engine. **Growth** assets are players in their prime (ages 23–27) who are one catalyst away from re-rating into Blue-Chip pricing. Think: a winger at a mid-table Bundesliga club who is heavily linked with a **£80m** move to the Premier League.

*   **Risk Profile:** Moderate.
*   **Volatility:** **5-8%**.
*   **The Play:** Capital appreciation through "The Bridge" effect (transfer arbitrage).

The Strategy Lab identifies these players by their **Underlying Metrics (UM)**: Expected Goals (xG), Progressive Carries, and Pass Completion under pressure. When the token price hasn't yet caught up to the skyrocketing UM, you have an arbitrage opportunity.

🚀 **Scout:** Look for players in the final two years of their contract. The "Transfer Window Multiplier" hits hardest here.

## Risk Management: The Three-Stage Linear Progression

How do you actually execute this? Don't overcomplicate it. Follow the Stockballer 3-step process:

1.  **Deposit & Tier:** Determine your total liquidity. If you have £1,000, your "Moonshot" cap is strictly £100. No exceptions.
