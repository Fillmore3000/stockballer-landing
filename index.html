Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start investing

Is StockBaller legal? +
Yes. StockBaller operates as a tokenized trading platform, not gambling. You own real digital assets (ERC-1155 tokens) that represent shares in athlete performance. We're registered and compliant with applicable regulations.
How do I cash out my profits? +
You can sell your tokens anytime during market hours. Proceeds are converted to USDC (stablecoin) in your wallet, which you can withdraw to your bank account or crypto exchange. Typical withdrawal time is 1-3 business days.
What's the minimum investment? +
You can start with as little as $10. There's no maximum limit, but we recommend starting small and learning the platform before making larger investments.
How are token prices calculated? +
Token prices are determined by real match performance. Goals add +3%, assists +2%, clean sheets +2% (for defenders/goalkeepers). Negative events like red cards or own goals reduce value. Prices update after each match.
What happens if a player gets injured? +
Injured players don't accumulate new performance points, so their token price may decline due to inactivity. This is part of the riskâ€”just like real stocks, injuries affect value. You can sell at any time or hold until they return.
Is my investment secure? +
Your tokens are stored on the Base blockchain (Layer 2 Ethereum) in your own wallet. We never hold custody of your assets. All transactions are transparent and verifiable on-chain. Your investment is as secure as the blockchain itself.
